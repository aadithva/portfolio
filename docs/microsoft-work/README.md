## Role

**Product Designer, Microsoft Copilot — 2024 to present**

Aadith works on the experience of AI-generated responses across Microsoft 365. His work
connects three concerns:

1. Helping people understand where generated information came from.
2. Making response experiences easier to scan, verify, and act on.
3. Creating repeatable ways to evaluate and improve AI UX quality.

## Portfolio narrative

The strongest narrative is not a list of isolated Copilot features. It is the evolution
of a design practice:

1. **Make AI output understandable.** Connect generated content to sources through clear
   citation and reference patterns.
2. **Make verification lightweight.** Preserve reading flow while giving people access
   to supporting context.
3. **Make quality measurable.** Translate design judgment into reusable criteria,
   examples, and regression checks.
4. **Make craft executable.** Move from static specifications toward prototypes and
   low-risk implementation contributions.
5. **Make the practice repeatable.** Share methods through reviews, workshops, and
   internal design enablement.
