## Preferred case-study framing

**Problem:** AI answers are only useful when people can understand their basis, inspect
supporting evidence, and judge quality.

**Contribution:** Designed source-transparency and response patterns, contributed to
response modernization, and co-created a repeatable UX quality-evaluation system.

**Outcome:** The evaluation framework became operational and surfaced quality issues;
response and craft improvements shipped through cross-functional product work.

**Working style:** Systems thinking across interaction design, information hierarchy,
evaluation, accessibility, and implementation.
