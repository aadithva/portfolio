- Credited collaborators: none
- Written case-study text: none
- Confirmed format: 24 image/GIF modules, one embedded video, and one YouTube link
- Classification: self-directed concept unless another source proves a MetaMask client
  relationship

### What the source supports

- A visual advertising system rather than one standalone poster.
- Multiple wide banner-format compositions.
- Tall system or flow presentations.
- Two animated ad units.
- At least one accompanying video or motion piece.

### What the source does not support

- A commissioned relationship with MetaMask or ConsenSys.
- A formal client brief, target audience, campaign KPI, or business outcome.
- User research, testing, or launch results.
- Claims that the work improved trust, adoption, or conversion.
